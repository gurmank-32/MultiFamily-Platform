# How to Add Credits to Your OpenAI Account

## Step-by-Step Instructions

### Step 1: Go to Billing Page

1. Open your web browser
2. Go to: **https://platform.openai.com/settings/organization/billing/overview**
   - Or: Log into https://platform.openai.com → Click your profile → Settings → Billing

### Step 2: Add Payment Method

1. On the billing page, you'll see:
   - Your current balance (probably $0.00)
   - A button that says **"Add payment method"** or **"Set up paid account"**

2. Click **"Add payment method"** or **"Set up paid account"**

3. You'll be asked to enter:
   - **Credit/Debit Card Information:**
     - Card number
     - Expiration date
     - CVV (3-digit security code)
     - Cardholder name
     - Billing address
   - OR
   - **PayPal** (if available in your region)

### Step 3: Add Initial Credits

1. After adding your payment method, you'll see options to add credits
2. **Recommended amount:** Start with **$5-10**
   - This is just a prepaid balance
   - You only pay for what you use
   - Very affordable for testing

3. Click **"Add $5"** or **"Add $10"** (or enter a custom amount)

4. Confirm the payment

### Step 4: Verify Credits Added

1. After payment, you should see:
   - Your new balance (e.g., "$10.00")
   - A confirmation message

2. You're all set! The credits are immediately available

---

## Alternative: Pay-As-You-Go Setup

Some accounts may use automatic billing instead of prepaid credits:

1. Go to billing settings
2. Enable **"Auto-recharge"** or **"Pay-as-you-go"**
3. Set a spending limit (e.g., $10/month)
4. Your card will be charged automatically when you use the API

## Troubleshooting

### "Payment method declined"
- Check your card has sufficient funds
- Verify billing address matches card
- Try a different card
- Contact your bank if issues persist

### "Can't add payment method"
- Make sure you're logged into the correct account
- Try a different browser
- Clear browser cache and try again
- Contact OpenAI support: https://help.openai.com/

### "Credits not showing up"
- Wait a few minutes (can take 1-2 minutes to process)
- Refresh the page
- Check your email for confirmation
- Contact OpenAI support if still not showing

## Setting Spending Limits (Optional but Recommended)

To avoid unexpected charges:

1. Go to: **https://platform.openai.com/settings/organization/limits**
2. Set a **monthly spending limit** (e.g., $20/month)
3. You'll get notified when you approach the limit
4. API calls will stop if you hit the limit
